Privacy Policy for Vaulted Coordinates

Last updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Vaulted Coordinates. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our app.

Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage Only

  • We do not collect any data from your device
  • All coordinate card data is stored locally on your device
  • Data is encrypted using military-grade encryption
  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No data is transmitted to any servers

Biometric Authentication

  • We use Face ID/Touch ID for authentication purposes only
  • We do not store any biometric data
  • Biometric authentication is handled by your device's secure system

Camera Usage

  • The camera is used only for scanning Bankinter coordinate cards
  • All image processing is done locally on your device
  • No images are stored or transmitted

How Your Data is Protected

  • All data is stored locally on your device with military-grade encryption
  • Access to your data is protected by Face ID/Touch ID
  • No data is shared with any third parties
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access your stored coordinate data
  • Delete your stored coordinate data
  • Export your coordinate data
  • Withdraw consent for biometric authentication

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
